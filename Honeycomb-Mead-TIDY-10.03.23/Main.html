What is Mead?

Mead (Miodh in Irish) is the world's oldest alcoholic brew, the primary ingredient of this ancient drink is honey however this does not mean that all mead is as sweet as honey since it can be incredibly diverse - ranging from dry to sweet, and paring well with combinations of other ingredients, such as fruit or spices. Our award-winning mead is judged as among the best in the world, packed full of delicious flavours and embodying the modern, refined style of mead making. Our mead is made from pure raw honey and carefully picked berries, carefully crafted using our very own authentic recipes.
