Quotes

The beauty of quotes is that they allow us to glimpse into another minds and understand how they think and look at the world. A quote is a powerful tool for our mind, our soul, our spirit. A quote can sharpen your mind and give you an instant and rapid relief if your soul is down, in a dark time.

— Dan Millman

“You don’t have to control your thoughts. You just have to stop letting them control you.”

— Abertoli

— Linda Poindexter

— Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson

― John Green

“There is hope, even when your brain tells you there isn’t.”

― Rupi Kaur
